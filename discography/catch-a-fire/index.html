Catch a Fire

The Wailers

Year 1973Label IslandProducer chris-blackwell

The first international Wailers album. Chris Blackwell gave them four thousand pounds, let them deliver a finished tape, then sweetened it for rock radio. The lighter-album cover is one of the great record-package designs of the seventies.
