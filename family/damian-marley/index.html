Damian Robert Nesta Marley

Jr. GongGongzilla
Born 1978-07-21

Singer · producer · 5× Grammy winner · youngest son

Listen on Spotify →

Bob's youngest son with Cindy Breakspeare, Miss World 1976. Built a sound that hangs reggae on hip-hop's chassis — Welcome to Jamrock (2005) won two Grammys including Best Reggae Album. Distant Relatives (2010) with Nas is a top-five hip-hop-reggae crossover ever. Stony Hill (2017) won Best Reggae Album. Cofounder of Stony Hill Records with brother Stephen.
