Neville O'Riley Livingston

Born 1947-04-10Died 2021-03-02Place Kingston, Jamaica

Vocals, percussion — Wailer · the soul of the trio

Listen on Spotify →

Bob's stepbrother (Bunny's father partnered briefly with Bob's mother). Highest tenor in the original trio. Stayed home when Bob and Peter went international — refused to fly, refused to tour cold climates. Blackheart Man (1976) is one of the greatest reggae records ever made. Three solo Grammys.
