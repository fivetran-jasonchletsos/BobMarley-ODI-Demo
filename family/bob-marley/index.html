Robert Nesta Marley

Bob MarleyTuff GongSkipperNesta
Born 1945-02-06Died 1981-05-11Place Nine Mile, Saint Ann Parish, Jamaica

Lead vocals, rhythm guitar, songwriter — center

Listen on Spotify →

Center of gravity. Born in a hut in Nine Mile to a teenage mother and a white quartermaster who barely figured. Moved to Trench Town at twelve, met Bunny and Peter in a yard, and from there built the first sound that travelled reggae out of Kingston and into the planet's bloodstream. Married Rita at twenty-one, fathered eleven publicly-acknowledged children by seven women, survived an assassination attempt in 1976, headlined Zimbabwe's independence in 1980, and died of melanoma at thirty-six. Sold more than 75 million records on six studio albums; the seventh, Legend, is the best-selling reggae record ever made.
